About

- A passion for webdevelopment and a need for code.

I have always loved tech and the way it can improve our lives. When I started to write my own code I was hooked. In 2021 I decided to enroll at Malmös Yrkeshögskola, iOS & Android Development. And in 2022 I enrolled at Sundsgårdens Folkhögskola, It-Tech for women, an yrkesutbildning/vocational-university. To create something from your own lines of code is so satisfying. I have a big desire getting to know how stuff works & I always strive to learn something new. This quality comes handy in the Tech-World where theres a constant flow of new things. When I'm not behind the computer I like to paint. I also like to listen to podcasts & watch documentaries, mainly about topics like history, science & tech. When the weather allows I go for a ride on my motorcycle.
